# Approach Annunciators

## Description

These are all the annunciators related to the Approach flight phase. It will show the category of approach that the aircraft is capable of as well as the DH or MDA (if selected). These annunciators are independant from the airport and runway capabilities. 

---

### CAT 1

Displayed with white text. Tells the pilot that the aircraft is capable of CAT 1 approaches. (One autopilot active).

### CAT 2

Displayed with white text. Tells the pilot that the aircraft is capable of CAT 2 approaches. (One autopilot active).

### CAT 3

Displayed with white text. Tells the pilot that the aircraft is capable of CAT 3 approaches. (One autopilot active).

### SINGLE

Displayed with white text. Tells the pilot that the aircraft is capable of CAT 3 approaches, with fail passive condition. (One autopilot active).

### DUAL

Displayed with white text. Tells the pilot that the aircraft is capable of CAT 3 approaches, with fail operational condition. (Both autopilots active).

### MDA/MDH XXXX

Displayed in white text with blue numbers. MDA will display the BARO (decision altitude) that has been set in the APPR PERF page of the MCDU. This is the barometric altitude. MDH will display the DH (decision height) that has been set in the MCDU APPR PERF page This is the radio altitude. 

### DH XXXX

Displayed in white text with blue numbers. Displays the desicion height that is inserted into the APPR PERF page in the MCDU

### NO DH

Displayed with white text. Displayed when MDA/MDH or DH is not inserted into the APPR PERF page of the MCDU.
